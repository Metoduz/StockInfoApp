# Product Overview

## Stock Info App

A Flutter-based mobile application for tracking and analyzing stock portfolio information. The app displays stock data including current prices, daily changes, and trading hints/analysis for various stocks.

### Key Features

- **Stock Portfolio Display**: Shows a list of stocks with current values, daily changes, and percentage movements
- **Stock Analysis Hints**: Provides trading insights including buy zones, support/resistance levels, and trend analysis
- **Interactive Stock Cards**: Expandable cards showing detailed stock information and hints
- **Alerts and Highlighting**: Highlighting cards and sending alerts on matching trading patterns or reaching indicator goals
- **News Page**: Page for news and trading suggestions 
- **Multi-platform Support**: Built with Flutter for iOS, Android, Web, Windows, macOS, and Linux

### Target Use Case

Personal stock portfolio tracking with basic technical analysis hints to help users make informed trading decisions. The app focuses on displaying stock information and trading strategy analytics with currency conversion support.
Using remote API calls, the app is able to send alerts to the user on reaching any of their trading strategy indicators. Based on their watchlist, the users also get individual news and analytics for their assets. Users are also able to track their buy/sell calls and get an overall analytics of their trading.

### Target Users
The target users are private investors that use their own money to invest. Therefor the UI should be simple to understand with a modern look.
